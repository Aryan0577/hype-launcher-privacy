Hype Launcher – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 August 2025

Hype Launcher (“the App”) is developed and maintained by Aryan Srivastava & Aryan Poonia (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from your device.

The App requests the following Android permissions solely to provide its features:

  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS – to display your app usage statistics (requires you to grant access in system settings).
  • INTERNET – for online integrations such as fetching coding challenge data (e.g., LeetCode, Codeforces).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to send notifications for to-do list reminders or updates.
  • EXPAND_STATUS_BAR – to expand or collapse the status bar as part of quick access gestures.

All features operate locally on your device. We do not send your usage data or personal information to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party data collection tools.

3. Data Retention

We do not collect or retain any personal data.

4. Security

All app settings and usage data remain stored locally on your device. You are responsible for securing your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated “Last updated” date.
